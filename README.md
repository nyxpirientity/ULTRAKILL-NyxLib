# About
**ULTRAKILL Additions I Want** is a janky mod made for my own personal use meant for ULTRAKILL and built for *Melon Loader* currently. This means the standard ULTRAKILL mod loader setup WILL (probably) NOT work for this code in it's current state and 
instead it'd need to be ported over. Although, I've heard about a melonloader -> bepinex compatibility layer, but I've not tested it and have no knowledge of it, so that may work. Alternatively, you can use Melonloader which is here: https://github.com/LavaGang/MelonLoader

# Cheats
It features mainly a variety of cheats that change the game up in random ways. Cheats are *intended* to be capable of being fully mixable, within reason, however, they may have bugs/issues ESPECIALLY when combined. Generally, I've found them good enough for challenge runs, even if still not as stable as I'd prefer. I'll list the cheats here:

## CHALLENGES:
- Enemies are Fueled with Blood - Enemies will consume YOUR blood when YOU'RE hit, and use it to heal...
- Give Enemies Friend(s) - for each enemy that spawns, clones will be made based on what you setup in a preferences file
- mundanemurder Mode - You've heard of ULTRAKILL, now get ready for mundanemurder, play mundanely, not stylishly.
- Hydra Mode - when you kill one enemy, they clone into two halved health enemies. Kill the clones within a configured period of time or else they'll clone again when killed...
- Salty Enemies - do more stylishly, and enemies stats change depending on the configuration in the preferences file
- Bad Gyro - V1's internal orientation sensing is malfunctioning, and thus your viewing angle will annoyingly sway around. What a shame.
- Self Consciense - V1 is self conscious, and thus depending on your style (and configuration) V1 will have varying amounts of energy. By default, lowest style rank causes your stamina to be _halved_
- Salty Enemies - Enemies will get very, very ANGRY the more style you get, (you get to set the radiance buff they get for each style rank in the prefs file) and those that can, will become enraged if you reach ULTRAKILL rank
- Heck Puppets - Heck itself will summon puppets to add to the chaos depending on your style rank, and configuration. By default, they should be noticably slower and lower in health, acting as both a hinderence and an advantage
- Heat of Heck - Heck itself will ALSO challenge V1 by literally BURNING them if they don't keep moving. The more style you have, the more rapidly the heat will consume you. When you're burning, your hard damage will rise, however, if your internal temperature is high, your external temperature is higher. Touching oil will ignite it, enemies will be hurt upon contact with you and many shots you fire will be so hot that they'll literally explode upon impact with an enemy. However, if you're shot, you'll also explode, taking more damage and messing up your movement. Currently, velocity from trams and rocket riding is NOT counted.
- Aggressive Agony - As enemies in the level suffer, both physically, and mentally (watching those around them get destroyed), the PAIN meter will rise, heck will use the PAIN you've caused to summon attacks against you, adding chaos. PAIN is NOT just a simple more damage = more pain, whilst I'm sure the formula I use could be better, finishing an enemy off faster actually will reduce the amount of PAIN, compared to slowly hurting them until their health finally drains to 0.
  
- Cybergrind Challenge - Cycles between pre-set challenges for Cybergrind, each wave, using these cheats! (like, combining cheats together to make challenges)

## SILLY:
- Radiance For Thyself - boosts ya stats
- ULTRASTOP - (almost) all sound effects played cause hitstop (flashing lights warning!)
  
## UTILITY:
- Override Cybergrind Starting Wave - overrides the starting wave for cybergrind, using a value set in the preferences file. (not currently functional!)
- Cybergrind Quick Restart - instantly restarts cybergrind without you actually dying when you die
- Disable Stops - Disables hitstops
- Disable Slowdown - Disables slowdown
- Immortality - Makes you immortal in that you'll get hit but never die (so, stay at at least 1 HP) (not currently functional!)
- Shortned Hitstop - halves hitstop time
- Always Play Battle Music - the battle never stops (for the music at least)
- Play Clean Music with Battle Music - Plays the calm/"clean" music alongside battle music
- No Corpses - enemies shouldn't have a corpse when they die (not currently function I think?)
